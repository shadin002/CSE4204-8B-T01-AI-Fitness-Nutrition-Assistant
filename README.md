## Project Description

AI Fitness & Nutrition Assistant is designed to help people follow a healthier lifestyle through fitness by using a proper guideline. This platform uses a person’s age, height, weight, current fitness goals, daily activity levels and even their budget on fitness to develop a personal training plan and dietary recommendations. Additionally, this service tracks a user's progress over time and gives them updated training plans and diet recommendations to help them remain committed to achieve a healthy lifestyle.
